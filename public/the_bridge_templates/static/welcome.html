openMarketplace helps you find the tools you need

It's an app store for impact-first organizations. Search, filter, compare features and price, see real usage patterns and reviews. Finding the right Android data capture tool (or SMS platform, MIS, visualization aid, etc.) to fit your organization's needs just got a lot easier.

openIntegrations makes apps more powerful

Need surveys captured with ODK to show up in your Salesforce system? Want MPESA payments to sync with your clinic management platform? Don't spend money on custom code. Make it happen with a simple drag-and-drop interface. With a totally open-source and modular design, the number of endpoints is growing.

openIdeas promotes conversations about technology's role in behavior change

75% of IT implementations fail. Want to learn from developers who abhore custom-coded solutions? Or CIOs who understand that culture is what makes or breaks an implementation? Want to learn how and why real organizations are both succeeding and failing to implement technologies geared at growing their social impact? Join the conversation.
