Technology isn't the answer,
but it shouldn't be the problem.

openMarketplace helps you find the tools you need.

It's an app store for impact-first organizations. Search, filter, compare features and prices, see real usage patterns and reviews. Finding the right Android data capture tool (or SMS platform, MIS, visualization aid, etc.) to fit your organization's needs just got a lot easier.

openIntegrations make
apps more powerful

It's a bridge that connects your data across your technologies. Need surveys captured with ODK to show up in your Salesforce system? Want MPESA payments to sync with your clinic management platform? Don't spend money on custom code. Make it happen with a simple drag-and-drop wizard. With an open-source, efficient, modular design, the number of endpoints will continue to grow. Login from the menu to learn more.

openIdeas promote conversations
about technology's role in behavior change

75% of IT implementations fail. Want to learn from developers who abhor custom-coded solutions? Or CIOs who understand that culture is what makes or breaks a technology project? Want to learn how and why real organizations are both succeeding and failing to implement technologies geared at growing their social impact? Join the conversation.
