*metadata layer
  • Describe
  • ++
  • A call to your server, asking for a description of a particular user's forms, objects, tables, etc.




*data layer
  • Listen API
  • If you can't do push notifications, let's set up a pull. We've got background workers scheduled to call out to your DB and fetch new records.
  • Serialize to JSON
  • ++
  • Once data from your app reaches us, it must be translated to our universal syntax.
  • Map
  • Your data is passed through a mapping, linking particular data points to tables and fields on someone else's app to create a payload.
  • Deserialize from JSON
  • ++
  • You take our payload, in the universal JSON, and convert it back to whatever someone else's app wants to read.
