openMarketplace is an ‘AppStore’ for social-impact technologies. Users are guided right to the technologies they need and will be able to access real usage statistics, reviews, ratings, and in-depth case studies on best practices for implementation.



openIntegrations makes technologies more powerful by connecting them. An SMS can automatically register a patient in a clinic system. A mobile payment can trigger the digital delivery of educational content. A mobile survey submission can be instantly mapped on the web. When integration is this easy, we will start to see an entirely new generation of focused, powerful, and user-friendly ‘apps’ be built to serve the social sector.
